Parts of the Paramecium
Cilia: Flexible projections extending outward from a cell that enable locomotion by whiplike motion.
Food: Provide energy for the cell to stay functioning.
Cytoplasm: Fluid inside the nucleus.
Gullet: The place in the cell where the food enters.
Nucleus: Contains the genetic programming information for the cell.
Food Vacuole: Membrane that stores food for the cell.
Contractile Vacuole: Removes excess water from the cell.
